Routine

Before & After Your Set

Before You Arrive

  • Come with clean, product-free lashes — no mascara, eyeliner, or glue.
  • Avoid caffeine right before your appointment; it can cause eye flutter.
  • Please arrive solo — no extra guests or little ones.
  • Wear glasses instead of contacts if your eyes are sensitive.

Caring For Your Lashes

  • Keep lashes completely dry for the first 24 hours.
  • Cleanse daily with a lash-safe cleanser to remove oil & buildup.
  • Avoid oil-based makeup removers and skincare near the eyes.
  • Brush daily with a spoolie and book fills every 2–3 weeks.
